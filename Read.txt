This repository centralizes the project management information for the Nueva EPS – PoC Migration from Red Hat Fuse On-Premise to ROSA project. It is intended to support the tracking of planning activities, responsibilities, progress, risks, commitments, costs, and supporting documentation related to the project execution.

Its purpose is to facilitate project administration from a PM perspective, ensuring traceability, deliverable control, meeting follow-up, dependency management, and overall visibility of the project status for the Blend team.
